Automated Arrhythmia Detection

Deep‑learning models tuned on long‑form recordings detect AFib, PVCs, AV block and more, with beat‑level outputs for review.

Clinician‑Style Summaries

Structured findings are transformed into clear, physician‑readable summaries to accelerate reporting and workflow.

HIPAA‑Ready Infrastructure

Encryption at rest & in transit, least‑privilege access, audit logs, and BAA availability on HIPAA‑eligible AWS services.

How it works

  1. Upload ECG via secure portal or API (bulk & streaming supported).
  2. Inference on managed endpoints (right‑sized CPU/GPU with autoscale).
  3. Review beat‑level annotations and episode summaries in the dashboard.
  4. Report export in PDF/JSON with full traceability.

Technical stack

  • AWS SageMaker for training & real‑time inference
  • Amazon S3 with KMS keys and VPC endpoints
  • EventBridge for scheduled retraining
  • CloudWatch for monitoring & drift alerts
  • Bedrock LLMs for narrative summaries

Compliance

We follow best practices for PHI: store PHI only in HIPAA‑eligible services, encrypt data at rest and in transit, maintain access controls and audit logging, and execute a Business Associate Addendum (BAA) upon request.
